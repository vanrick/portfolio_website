About

My family is from Vietnam, We moved to Denver with hopes and dreams of working and finding a better future. We were drawn to Denver for the combination of city, mountains, and culture, we quickly found ourselves living in a 3-bedroom apartment with 7 people sharing 2 bathrooms. We needed a family computer for homework and studies since we couldnâ€™t afford a computer; I decided to build my own by slowly piecing together parts. Through my struggles of learning how to build a computer sparked a lifetime interest in technology.
While working at US Bank as a Senior Banker, I started to fix phones and other gizmos as a hobby for other people. I was hungry to learn how to code software and began teaching myself with online courses. I decided to enroll in the Galvanize Full Stack Web Development program. This fully immersive program has exposed me to many cutting-edge technologies, coding fundamentals and how to implement them. Coding and technology are one of my life passions. I'm excited to be a lifelong learner of the craft and be a part of the community.
