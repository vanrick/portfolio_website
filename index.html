About

From Upstate New York, I moved to Denver February 2015 with dreams of working for a tech startup. Drawn to Denver for the combination city, mountains and booming tech scene, I quickly found myself working Signpost a Google Ventures backed startup. While at Signpost I became more interested in the coding aspect of software and began teaching myself with online courses. After being a top sales performer and champion of their patented CRM for a year. I decided to enroll in the Galvanize Full Stack Web Development program. This fully immersive program has exposed me to many cutting edge technologies, coding fundamentals and how to implement them. Coding has become one of my life passions. I'm excited to be a lifelong learner of the craft and be a part of the community.
